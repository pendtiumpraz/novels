## Aturan Semi-Realistic:
1. **Gunakan kata kunci:** "semi-realistic", "cinematic lighting", "detailed face", "professional concept art", "photorealistic textures"
2. **JANGAN gunakan:** "anime", "manga", "cel shaded", "cartoon", "2d art"
3. **Wajib:** "Indonesia", Southeast Asian features, brown skin
4. **Referensi visual:** Kayak cutscene game RPG keren (The Witcher, Final Fantasy, Genshin Impact, Black Myth Wukong)
5. **Warna karakter tetap konsisten:** Arya = biru tua, Bima = putih emas, Chandra = hitam abu
